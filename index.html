Welcome to Nature Nest!

"...where the enchanting beauty of Kerala unfolds before your eyes. Nestled in the southwestern coast of India, Kerala is a tropical paradise that captivates with its lush landscapes, serene backwaters, and pristine beaches. Known as 'God's Own Country,' this vibrant state is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry, from traditional Kathakali performances to colorful festivals that celebrate the spirit of Kerala. Explore the tranquil backwaters on a houseboat or unwind on the golden shores of Kovalam. With its diverse flora and fauna, historic monuments, and warm hospitality, Kerala beckons you to experience a harmonious blend of nature and culture. Come, embark on a journey where every moment is a celebration of the extraordinary in the lap of Mother Nature.".

Alappuzha

The "Venice of the East" offers serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and the famous Nehru Trophy Boat Race, making it a tranquil gem in God's Own Country.

Munnar

A scenic hill station famous for tea plantations, misty hills, and stunning landscapes. Perfect for nature lovers seeking a serene retreat in God's Own Country

Kochi

A historic city on the Arabian Sea, renowned for its colonial architecture, iconic Chinese fishing nets, and vibrant culture.
